🏠 Neighborhood & Subway Tips

Heads Up: 🔇 The buzzer's on vacation. 🗑️ Toss trash daily to keep the creepy crawlies away. ♻️ Recyclables chill outside on the 3rd floor, and there's a magic chute for regular trash!

Neighbor Elena: 📞 1-347-856-7222 (5th floor) - ask her to take you on the roof!

Your Ticket to Adventure: 🚇 Q Train

Q Train Quest: Head east on E 15th St towards Avenue J. Turn left onto Avenue J and walk about 0.2 miles. The Q train station (Avenue J) will be on your right. Look for the subway entrance between East 15th and East 16th Streets.

Train Direction Tips:
👉 For Manhattan: Take the Q train northbound (towards 96 St-2 Av).
👉 For Coney Island: Take the Q train southbound (towards Coney Island-Stillwell Av). I recommend you take a walk on the Coney Island Boardwalk while you're here! it's a short ride.

🚢 Manhattan Excursions

🏝️ Governor's Island

A car-free oasis just a short ferry ride away! It's like a mini-vacation from your vacation.

Ferry Facts: 🎟️ $2.50 for a scenic ride. On weekday mornings, snag a free bike rental for an hour!

How to Get There:
1. Hop on the Q train northbound (towards 96 St-2 Av) to Atlantic Ave-Barclays Ctr.
2. Transfer to the 4 or 5 train southbound (towards Bowling Green).
3. Get off at Bowling Green and follow signs to the ferry terminal.
4. Enjoy your boat ride to island bliss! 🚢

🌉 Brooklyn Bridge Walk

A stroll so nice, they wrote songs about it! Don't forget your camera - this view is Instagram gold!

Walking the Walk:
1. Take the Q train northbound (towards 96 St-2 Av) to DeKalb Ave.
2. Follow the signs (or the crowd) to the Brooklyn Bridge Pedestrian Walkway.
3. Walk towards Manhattan for the classic view, or towards Brooklyn for a fresh perspective!
